Rich Dad Poor Dad

Robert

"Rich Dad Poor Dad" by Robert Kiyosaki is a fascinating read that contrasts the financial wisdom of two father figures in the author's life. One is his real dad, the "Poor Dad," who emphasizes traditional education and job security. The other is his best friend's dad, the "Rich Dad," who teaches him about financial independence, investing, and entrepreneurship. The book is a compelling guide to understanding money and building wealth.
